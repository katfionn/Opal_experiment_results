分析框架与方法论概述

数据收集 (Data Collection)

Custom URLs are acquired via direct user submission. Content extraction from these URLs employs a dedicated web scraping module designed to bypass common anti-bot measures and handle dynamic JavaScript content. Content is restricted to publicly accessible text data, excluding paywalled or login-protected resources.

预处理 (Pre-processing)

Extracted text undergoes several stages: HTML tag removal, noise reduction (advertisements, navigation elements), language identification, text normalization (lowercase conversion, punctuation removal), stop-word filtering, and lemmatization. Non-English content is flagged and, if possible, translated using an integrated translation service before further processing, or excluded based on user-defined language scope.

情感分析 (Sentiment Analysis)

Sentiment is determined using a hybrid approach combining a fine-tuned transformer-based deep learning model for nuanced sentiment detection (positive, negative, neutral, mixed) with a lexicon-based system for specific emotional valence identification (e.g., anger, joy). Results are quantified as a polarity score ranging from -1 to 1 and a subjectivity score from 0 to 1.

主题建模 (Topic Modeling)

Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) and Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF) are applied to identify overarching themes and sub-topics within the aggregated custom URL data. Topics are defined by clusters of co-occurring keywords, with their prevalence and evolution tracked over time.

讨论趋势识别 (Discussion Trend Identification)

Trends are identified through a combination of temporal keyword frequency analysis, burst detection algorithms for sudden increases in topic mentions, and anomaly detection for unusual shifts in sentiment or topic distribution. Trends are categorized by their velocity, duration, and associated sentiment.

社会群体视角区分 (Social Group Perspective Distinction)

Different social group perspectives are distinguished by clustering user-generated content (where applicable and available) based on inferred demographic markers (e.g., from public profiles, content style) and explicit stance detection on specific issues. This allows for the segmentation of opinions and the identification of divergent viewpoints across various inferred cohorts. (Attributed to 'Public Opinion Analysis Framework')

报告结构与发现标准 (Reporting Structure and Strict Criteria for Findings)

Findings are presented in a standardized report structure comprising an executive summary, detailed methodology, core insights (segmented by sentiment, topic, and trend), and actionable recommendations. Strict criteria for reporting findings include: statistical significance (p-value < 0.05 for quantitative claims), consistent trend observation over a minimum defined period, and corroboration across multiple data points or analytical methods. All reported insights must be traceable to raw data excerpts. (Attributed to 'Reporting and Synthesis Mechanism')

URL处理 (URL Handling)

Custom URLs are subjected to a validation process to ensure they are well-formed and accessible. A dedicated crawler module then fetches content, prioritizing text over media. URLs are cached for efficiency, and metadata (e.g., publication date, author, domain authority) is extracted and stored. Redundant or duplicate URLs are identified and processed only once, with content updates monitored based on a specified refresh interval. (Attributed to 'URL Source Acquisition and Management')
